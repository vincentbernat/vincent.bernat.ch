It was presented during an [eValais.ch session][] around the language, in Martigny, Switzerland. At the time, I was seduced by the good parts, including the tooling and the ecosystem. However, there were several bad parts: `GOPATH`, no generics, no versioning, and bad and unstable dependency management. With the release of [Go 1.18 supporting generics][], all these are now solved! ![Slides in PDF format]([[!!files/slides-go-exoscale.pdf]]) [eValais.ch session]: http://www.evalais.ch/27-03-2018-session-evalais-ch-langage-de-programmation-go/ [Go 1.18 supporting generics]: https://go.dev/doc/go1.18#generics [Exoscale]: https://www.exoscale.com
